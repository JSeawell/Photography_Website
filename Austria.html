Innsbruck, Austria

Innsbruck Austria was the first stop on my BusAbout journey. When we arrived by bus, the skies were dark and foreboding, and the beautiful Austrian Alps were nowhere to be seen. I was so bummed, and I worried that in my short time in Innsbruck, I would miss all the beauty I had hoped for.

Thankfully, I couldn't have been more wrong. I woke up the next moring to sun pouring through the window shutters, and I hurried outside. As I looked around, I felt dwarfed by the towering Appalachians. What a sight. In the couple of days I spent in Innsbruck, I was able to walk the outskirts of the city, and then take a tram up the side of the giant mountains. The views at the top were out of this world. They did not look real. I even feel like my photographs look fake, and while they are true to life, they hardly capture the awe.
