About Me

Hi! My Name is Jake Seawell. I was born in the PNW 27 years ago, and I've had an adventurous childhood.I grew up in Hillsboro, graduated from Hilhi in 2009, and left for the University of Colorado at Boulder to study Aerospace Engineering. I spent a year and a half in Engineering and AFROTC before deciding I wasn't enjoying my path.

I transferred back home and enrolled at Oregon State University, where I earned a bachelor's of science in The Psychology of Creative Writing. I continued my education with a Master's of Arts in Teaching from Pacific University, and received endorsements in English Language Arts and Advanced Mathematics. I spent the next three years teaching and coaching at Union High School, in Vancouver/Camas, WA.

Teaching was not for me, at least not yet, so I decided to resign and pursue another degree. Which leads me to my current pursuit. I am now enrolled online at Oregon State studying Computer Science. My dream job would be to work in the field of Sports Analysis, but Aerospace Technology would also be incredible.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking to beautiful locations, drone/landscape photography, playing/watching/coaching/umpiring sports, spending time with my family and friends, and playing with my stubborn German Shepherd, Odin. He turns 1 soon :)

I dream of a fullfilling job, a large and loving family of my own, travel, adventure, and hoards of sports. My life has been quite an adventure, but hopefully an adventure that has only just begun.
